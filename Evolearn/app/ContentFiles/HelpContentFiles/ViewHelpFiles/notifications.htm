Notifications

~~~~~~

Here you will find all of the notifications send by the Teacher.

You have the ability to clear all current notifications by clicking or fetch all available notification from the server, assuming of course that you have a working connection to said server by clicking

When you have notifications, they are ordered from newest to oldest.
If you missed any notifications, you will see a counter in the notifications section of the menu.
When you click on notifications, you will see all the missed notifications covered by a green border, which means that they are unseen.
Next time you open the notifications view they will lose the green border, which means that they are now seen.
By clicking on the notification you can get to see how it would look if you had just received it.
You can delete a specific notification by clicking

You also have the ability to not show a modal window any time you receive a new notification by enabling Do Not Disturb Mode in the settings.
In this mode, any new notifications will be saved here as seen and the notification counter in the menu will show how many you received.
