Quiz Review

~~~~~~

Here you are able see the correct answers to the given quiz, assuming you have successfully fulfilled the requirements to unlock it.

But since you are reading this, you do not have to worry about that.

If the question has an explanation reference you can click it, and be taken to the appropriate document.

Assuming the reference is fully written, you will also be navigated to the correct page, as well as have the answer highlighted in the text with a yellow background.

Other than that all the other controls are exactly the same as the regular quiz view.
